About Me

I'm a Computer Science student at UC Irvine with a deep passion for artificial intelligence, machine learning, and building systems that are both technically robust and human-centered.

My journey in computer science is driven by curiosity about how intelligent systems work and how we can make them safer, more reliable, and more beneficial for society. I specialize in AI safety research, model optimization, and bridging complex data systems with intuitive user experiences.

Through my research and professional experiences, I've worked on everything from red-teaming frontier language models to building conversational reporting tools, always with a focus on understanding both the technical depth and the human impact of the systems I create.

When I'm not coding or researching, I enjoy tutoring mathematics, reading about the latest developments in AI safety, and exploring the intersection of technology and design.
